Functionality vs Function

In a lot of papers or technical articles, the word “functionality” always shows up. This sometimes makes people like me who is not a native speaker confused about the difference between functionality and function. So, what is the difference?

From the sufix “ity”, we may deduce that “functionality” maybe relate to “ability”. and yes. From Wikidiff, we have:

  • Functionality
    • The ability to perform a task or function; that set of functions that something is able or equipped to perform.
  • Function
    • What something does or is used for
